About Our General Medicine Department

Our General Medicine department provides comprehensive primary care services for adults, focusing on prevention, early detection, and management of acute and chronic conditions. We serve as your first point of contact for health concerns and coordinate all aspects of your care.

Under the leadership of Dr. Sarah Thompson, our team of internists provides personalized, evidence-based care with a focus on building long-term patient-doctor relationships.

  • Preventive Care: Annual physicals and health screenings
  • Chronic Disease Management: For diabetes, hypertension, and more
  • Acute Care: Treatment for illnesses and minor injuries
