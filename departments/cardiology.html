About Our Cardiology Center

Our cardiology department is a leader in cardiovascular care, offering state-of-the-art diagnostics, interventional procedures, and rehabilitation programs. We are committed to preventing, diagnosing, and treating heart disease with personalized approaches.

Our Treatments & Procedures

We offer comprehensive cardiovascular treatments using the latest evidence-based approaches.

Coronary Angiography

Minimally invasive imaging to visualize coronary arteries and identify blockages or narrowing.

Outpatient 30-60 Minutes

Angioplasty & Stenting

Emergency procedure to open blocked arteries during a heart attack and restore blood flow.

Time-sensitive

Pacemaker Implantation

Surgical procedure to implant a device that regulates abnormal heart rhythms.

Inpatient 1-2 Day Stay

Echocardiography

Non-invasive ultrasound imaging to evaluate heart structure and function.

Outpatient

Ablation Therapy

Procedure to correct abnormal heart rhythms by scarring small areas of heart tissue.

2-4 Hour Procedure

Cardiac Rehabilitation

Supervised program of exercise and education to improve cardiovascular health.

12-36 Sessions
