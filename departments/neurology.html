Our Treatments & Procedures

We offer comprehensive neurological treatments using the latest evidence-based approaches.

EEG Testing

Electroencephalogram to evaluate electrical activity in the brain for seizure diagnosis and monitoring.

Outpatient 60-90 Minutes

Thrombolytic Therapy

Emergency clot-busting medication for acute ischemic stroke to restore blood flow to the brain.

Time-sensitive

Deep Brain Stimulation

Surgical implantation of a neurostimulator to treat movement disorders like Parkinson's disease.

Inpatient 2-3 Day Stay

Botox Therapy

Injections to treat chronic migraine, muscle spasticity, and certain movement disorders.

Outpatient
