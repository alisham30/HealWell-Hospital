About HealWell Hospital

Our Humble Beginnings

Founded in 1995 by Dr. Sarah Thompson and a team of passionate healthcare professionals, HealWell Hospital started as a small 20-bed community clinic with a dream to redefine healthcare delivery. What began as a modest facility in downtown Chicago has now blossomed into a premier healthcare institution recognized nationally for its patient-centered approach.

Our journey has been marked by steady growth, technological adoption, and an unwavering commitment to medical excellence. From our first successful open-heart surgery in 1998 to becoming a Level I Trauma Center in 2010, each milestone has strengthened our resolve to serve the community better.

Today, we stand as a 500-bed multi-specialty quaternary care hospital with advanced medical facilities, cutting-edge technology, and a team of highly skilled professionals dedicated to providing compassionate, comprehensive patient care.

Our Core Philosophy

"To heal with compassion, to innovate with purpose, and to serve with excellence - these principles guide every decision we make and every life we touch at HealWell Hospital."

Our Mission & Vision

At HealWell Hospital, our mission is to deliver world-class medical care through innovation, expertise, and compassion, ensuring every patient receives personalized treatment tailored to their unique needs.

We envision a future where healthcare transcends boundaries - where advanced medicine meets human touch, where technology enhances rather than replaces the healing relationship, and where every individual has access to quality care regardless of their background or circumstances.

Our core values of Integrity, Excellence, Compassion, Innovation, and Community guide our daily operations and long-term strategic decisions. These values are reflected in our patient satisfaction scores that consistently rank in the top 1% nationally.

Building the Future of Healthcare

As we look ahead, HealWell Hospital is committed to pioneering the next generation of medical care. Our ambitious expansion plan includes establishing specialized centers of excellence in oncology, neurology, and cardiac care across three new locations by 2025.

We're investing $200 million in telemedicine infrastructure to bring our expertise to underserved rural communities. Our research division is collaborating with leading universities on groundbreaking studies in regenerative medicine and AI-assisted diagnostics.

The upcoming HealWell Innovation Hub will serve as an incubator for medical startups, fostering collaboration between physicians, engineers, and entrepreneurs to solve healthcare's most pressing challenges.

Recognized Excellence

HealWell Hospital's commitment to quality has been recognized through numerous awards and accreditations, including:

  • Joint Commission International Accreditation (2018, 2022)
  • Magnet Recognition for Nursing Excellence (5 consecutive years)
  • Top 50 Cardiovascular Hospitals in the U.S. (2020-2023)
  • Best Patient Experience Award - National Healthcare Association

Our specialists have performed over 100,000 successful surgeries, published 250+ research papers, and pioneered 15 innovative surgical techniques now adopted worldwide.
