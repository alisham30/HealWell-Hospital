About HealWell Hospital

Founded in 1995, HealWell Hospital has grown to become a leading healthcare provider in the region. Our mission is to deliver exceptional patient care through innovation, expertise, and compassion.

With over 200 beds and 50 specialist departments, we serve more than 100,000 patients annually. Our hospital is accredited by the Joint Commission International, ensuring the highest standards of care.
